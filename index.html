WhatsApp Setup

01 Go to ultramsg.com and create a free account.
02 Create a new instance. Scan the QR code with your WhatsApp to connect your number.
03 From your dashboard, copy your Instance ID and Token. These stay on the server — never exposed in the browser.
04 Enter your phone (e.g. 2348012345678 — country code, no plus sign), Instance ID, Token, and your preferred send time in UTC. Nigeria is UTC+1, so 7:00 UTC = 8:00 AM Lagos time.
